Hello world!

CRUISE is a collaborative project initiated by DoSSIER student researchers. As a part of CRUISE, this search engine aims to help academics coming into a space understand how the concepts associated with a specific field relate to each other and give them some structuring of the information space.

DoSSIER is an EU Horizon 2020 ITN/ETN on Domain Specific Systems for Information Extraction and Retrieval. DoSSIER will elucidate, model, and address the different information needs of professional users.
